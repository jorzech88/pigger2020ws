Pigger 2021

It's not too early to mark your calanders for Pigger 2020!

Alcohol, fire, a lake. What could go wrong? - Joe Santos



Due to ongoing health and safety issues, we regret to share that Pigger 2020 has been postponed until September 25, 2021. We will be working on setting up some virtual events, so stay tuned!

Pigger 2020 is being held on the grounds of the Magnuson Grand Hotel, located at exit 60 on I-55 and Illinois Route 108.

Tickets are $35, which includes food and drinks. Some of the drinks may include alcohol. Firepits and tiki torches will be set up for your enjoyment.

Pigger 2020 is part of a weekend of Blackburn Homecoming events:

  • Friday, September 25th, 2020
    • 10:00 am: BU Athletics Golf Outing at Carlinville Country Club
    • 7:00 pm: Friday night dance hosted by Mashaan Guy
    • Late-night Karaoke hosted by David Hofmann
  • Saturday, September 26th, 2020
    • 7:30 am: Annual Beaverthon 5K
    • 1:00 pm: Barney Dorm Crawl. Scavenger hunt.
    • 3:00 pm: 50th Anniversary Pigger!
  • Sunday, September 27th, 2020
    • 12:00 pm: Third Mass (The goodbye party) at the Anchor Inn.

This will be the largest gathering of Blackburn College alumni in history. It all happens on Blackburn's Homecoming weekend, September 25 through 27, 2020.

2017 was a huge hit, bringing 365 Blackburn College family members together. There were old alumni, young alumni, current students, retired faculty and current faculty. You name it.

Pigger 2017 was big. This one will be even bigger.

BU Althletic's Golf

2020 Blackburn College Althletics Golf Outing

The Blackburn College Athletic Department is excited to host the annual Beaver Backer Golf Scramble on Friday, September 25, 2020. We will again play at the Carlinville Country Club, just south of town on Illinois Route 4, the event will begin at 10:00 AM.

Friday Night Dance

Friday night Dance hosted by Mashaan Guy

Here's one of the BU family that really stepped up to the "reconnection" challenge. When Mashaan Guy heard of Pigger 2017, he joined in and volunteered to host an Old School Dance on Friday night as a kickoff to Pigger weekend. He found a place (The Magnuson Grand Hotel), found a DJ (Epic Entertainment), and created a great opportunity for all of us to party, like it's a "Dorm Event". I want to thank Mashaan for his commitment to what we are trying to do.

It will be back!

The ticket price is $10. There will be cash bar and appetizers and plenty of Alumni.

It is being held in the reception room, to the right as you enter the Magnuson Grand Hotel.

Make sure you have the Friday Night Dance on your Pigger 2020 itinerary!

Late Night Karaoke

Late night Karaoke hosted by David Hofmann

Warm up your vocal chords and get ready to sing the night away! Join us for karaoke after the Friday night dance. (Curling irons and sobriety optional.)
